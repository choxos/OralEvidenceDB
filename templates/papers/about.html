Technology & Innovation

OralEvidenceDB leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence and modern web technologies to transform how researchers access and analyze oral health literature.

AI-Powered PICO Extraction

Our system uses advanced Large Language Models (OpenAI GPT, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini) to automatically extract PICO elements from research abstracts, including Population, Intervention, Comparison, Outcome, Setting, Study Type, and Timeframe.

Comprehensive Database

Integrated with PubMed's E-utilities API to continuously import and index oral health research papers, providing researchers with up-to-date access to the latest evidence in stomatognathic diseases, dentistry, and oral health.

Advanced Search & Filtering

Sophisticated search capabilities allow researchers to filter studies by PICO elements, study types, publication years, journals, and more, enabling precise identification of relevant research.

VPS-Native Architecture

Built on modern infrastructure with Django, PostgreSQL, and deployed on custom VPS, ensuring scalability, reliability, and accessibility for researchers worldwide.
